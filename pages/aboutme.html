My bio

Hello, I'm Ana, a freelance digital illustrator with a background in digital marketing. My creative journey, which began in 2008, has taken me from the world of design and marketing to a wholehearted embrace of children's book illustration.

My experience includes crafting communication strategies and branding, creating captivating graphic pieces, and providing creative direction.

As a freelance illustrator, I've worked with a diverse range of clients, including small and medium-sized enterprises, where I've designed communication strategies, created advertising illustrations, and worked on character design for product packaging.

My transition to full-time children's book illustration is driven by a deep commitment to nurturing children's love for reading and sparking their imaginations.

I'm excited to share the magic of storytelling through my illustrations with you on this artistic journey. Thank you for joining me!
