Hi! My name is Ana

I'm a Freelance Illustrator born and raise in a valley surrounded by mountains in Mendoza, Argentina. Since I was a child, I always enjoyed drawing, so it was no surprise when I decided to study Graphic Design at the National University of Cuyo but finally ended up turning my heart to illustration. Nowadays I enjoy digitally illustrating subjects ranging from everyday situations, through fantasy and magic to science fiction oriented to a young audience.
